\end{align}
$p=100,  \sigma=\{0.5,1,2,3,5\}, cor(x_j,x_s)=\theta^{|j-s|}$

\bigskip

Estimation sample: 200.
Validation sample: 200.

\bigskip

Design 1: $\beta_j = 1\{j\leq 20\}, s=20.$\\
Design 2: $\beta_j = (0.5)^j.$
